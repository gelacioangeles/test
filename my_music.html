My Music

What is Zero?

Zero is my artist name. I use it whenever I am working on music. I have been creating and performing music for around 8-9 years now. The type of music I create is in the genre of Deathmetal, but I also do variations of metal or even different genres.

My Path Into Creating Music

I started playing guitar at a very young age. Over the years, I've also learned other instruments along the way, such as bass, drums, piano, and even vocals.
